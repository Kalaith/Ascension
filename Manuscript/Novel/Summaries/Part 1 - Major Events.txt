Major Events - Part 1

- Ksena's ascension ritual fails/resists expected control, making her a dangerous anomaly.
- Political pressure and fear drive exile/banishment decisions around Ksena and connected groups.
- Ksena and Alcuin are pushed into survival travel, including early combat and threat encounters.
- They investigate old locations (including tomb/ritual-linked places) for answers and shelter.
- Tribal and city cultural differences are explored, especially around technology and tradition.
- Growing conflict with city authority and ritual control systems raises the stakes for open resistance.
- Part 1 closes with stronger character bonds and a clearer path toward confrontation in Part 2.
