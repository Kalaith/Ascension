Major Events - Prequel

- Early conflict with foreign invaders/outsiders establishes the core threat to the world.
- The power imbalance between ordinary people and ascendant-level forces becomes clear.
- Foundational events set up later systems of control, fear, and survival politics.
- Key historical damage and losses create the long-term trauma referenced in later parts.
- The prequel ends by positioning the world for the rise of the city order and later resistance.
