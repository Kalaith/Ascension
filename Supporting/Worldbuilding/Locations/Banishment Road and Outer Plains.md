# Banishment Road and Outer Plains

## What It Is
- The wilderness corridor west of the city used immediately after exile.

## What We Know
- Terrain includes ridges, open plains, cave-mouth camps, stone folds, and predator-active areas.
- Survival pressure is constant: water scarcity, exposure, route uncertainty, and threat detection.
- Contains old trade markers and improvised camp chains.

## Major Events Here
- Ksena and Alcuin transition from shock to survival discipline.
- Decision to pursue hidden temple map leads.

## Narrative Role
- First major break from city conditioning into field autonomy and shared decision-making.
