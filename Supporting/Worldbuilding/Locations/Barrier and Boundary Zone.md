# Barrier and Boundary Zone

## What It Is
- The energy divide between city and outside world; includes locks, checkpoints, boundary stones, and maintenance cuts.

## What We Know
- Operationally enforced by city protocols and signatures.
- Used as both physical protection and political separation tool.
- Boundary procedures include compliance logs and controlled transfers.

## Major Events Here
- Ksena's repeated crossings in youth.
- Formal exile transfer of Ksena and forced companion exile of Alcuin.
- Later revelation that barrier control includes discriminatory and succession logic.

## End-State
- Barrier shell and control structure are dismantled/neutralized in the regime fall.
- City and village movement starts reopening under new terms.
