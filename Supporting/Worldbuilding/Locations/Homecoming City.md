# Homecoming City

## What It Is
- Later city stop characterized by practical architecture, strict civic protocols, and functioning maintenance systems.

## What We Know
- Explicit limits on access to military/water/vault sectors.
- Uses municipal guides and controlled movement passes.
- Supports independent historians (Halven) and civic operators (Ressa) who hold alternative records.

## Major Events Here
- Ksena obtains map and doctrine confirmations on distributed vs monopolized ascension governance.
- Gains route support documents for further travel.

## Narrative Role
- Provides institutional proof that non-monopoly models survived and remained governable.
