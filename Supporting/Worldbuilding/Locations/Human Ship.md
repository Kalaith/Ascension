# Human Ship

## What It Is
- Human military/science vessel used for contact, negotiation, medical support, and strategic planning.

## What We Know
- Includes command deck, guest quarters, medbay corridors, training bay, observation gallery, engineering archive lab, and shuttle interfaces.
- Operates under formal command/accountability constraints and mission audit expectations.
- Functions as neutral-ish coalition platform rather than local sovereign authority.

## Major Events Here
- First structured diplomacy between Ksena/Alcuin and General Hale.
- Intelligence fusion: satellite recon, terrain mapping, and conflict modeling.
- Later logistics support and envoy transition planning.

## Narrative Role
- Enables scale jump from fugitive survival to multi-party campaign coordination.
