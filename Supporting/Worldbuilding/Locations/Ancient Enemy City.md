# Ancient Enemy City

## What It Is
- Long-ruined former enemy city from ritual war narratives.

## What We Know
- Features collapsed towers, buried chambers, and still-monitored ruins.
- Ksena and Alcuin recover a cylindrical projector showing orbital vectors and defense arcs.
- Indicates the city participated in planetary defense, not only ground conflict.

## Major Events Here
- Contact with local scavenger confirms active patrol/scavenger interest.
- Evidence reframes war history: real ancient conflict, uncertain present threat framing.

## End-State
- Site remains a strategic evidence source and danger zone.
