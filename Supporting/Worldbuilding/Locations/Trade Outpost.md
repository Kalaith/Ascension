# Trade Outpost

## What It Is
- Multi-dialect frontier market and logistics node.

## What We Know
- Appears temporary at first glance but runs with stable routines.
- Dense practical economy: hunters, trappers, cart teams, fish/grain trade.
- Serves as a social-intelligence and route-planning hub.

## Major Events Here
- Ksena and Alcuin make contact with Estele.
- Transition from isolated survival to networked movement.
