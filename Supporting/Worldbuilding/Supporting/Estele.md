## Deep Dive

- Visual Markers: Practical gear-first style; adaptable to ship and ground environments.

Psychology and Temperament:
- Core Drives: Self-determination, competence, and refusal to be sidelined.
- Internal Conflict: Survival-first independence versus deepening loyalty to team outcomes.
- Leadership Style: Direct, unsentimental, highly adaptive under ambiguity.

Capabilities:
- Power/Skill Profile: Elite tracker; fast uptake in paired ascendant timing and decoy execution.
- Combat/Field: Route optimization, local contact calibration, stealth movement.
- Strategic Value: Converts uncertain terrain into actionable movement advantage.

Narrative Function:
- Evolves from transactional guide into trusted coalition operator and envoy bridge.
