# General Hale
Final Alignment: Supporting ally (conditional military partner)
Description:
- Human general balancing intervention risk, legitimacy, and civilian protection.
- Skeptical, disciplined commander who insists on legal/ethical framing.
Key Moments:
- Authorizes controlled recon instead of premature force projection.
- Negotiates cooperation terms with local coalition under contested trust.
- Oversees containment and coordinated operations against ascendant threats.
- Supports high-risk final operation planning with strict challenge review.
Major Interactions:
- Kirk: command channel for reconnaissance and operational execution.
- Ksena and Alcuin: alliance partner, sometimes cautious to the point of tension.
- Kalani: containment priority during escalation phase.
Final-Alignment Notes:
- Remains ally, but consistently framed as a bounded external force rather than local ruler.
## Deep Dive

Detailed Description:
- Physical Profile: Human baseline appearance; no ascended or alien phenotype, standard military command build.
- Visual Markers: Minimalist command presentation, fatigue visible but managed.
- Presence: Controlled intensity; every sentence framed for consequence.

Psychology and Temperament:
- Core Drives: Mission legitimacy, non-abusive intervention, and civilian harm reduction.
- Internal Conflict: Urgency versus uncertainty in first-contact intervention.
- Leadership Style: Challenge-review culture, legal framing, bounded force doctrine.

Capabilities:
- Power/Skill Profile: Strategic commander, high-level risk balancing, containment planning.
- Combat/Field: Delegates aggressively but retains accountability pressure at decision points.
- Strategic Value: Prevents coalition from trading one coercive regime for another.

Narrative Function:
- External power center that tests whether protagonists can build trustworthy alliances.
